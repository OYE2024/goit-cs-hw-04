One of the most significant trends is the widespread adoption of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML). AI is no longer just a buzzword; it has become an essential tool for businesses in various industries. Companies are leveraging AI for automation, decision-making, customer service, and data analysis. With the rise of generative AI, businesses can create new content, automate complex tasks, and enhance user experiences more efficiently than ever before. AI is also playing a crucial role in improving cybersecurity by detecting threats and preventing cyberattacks in real-time.
